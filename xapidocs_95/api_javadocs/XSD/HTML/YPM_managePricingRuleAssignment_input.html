PricingRuleAssignment Associated With Table: YPM_PRICING_RULE_ASSIGNMENT
Specify the pricing rule assignment information to be created, modified or deleted. An assignment is either for a specific customer or group of customers (based on the attributes of customer). Modifying a customer specific assignment by passing any of the customer attributes resets CustomerID and Shareable to null. Similarly modifying an assignment defined based on customer attributes, by passing CustomerID resets all the customer attributes on the assignment to null.

Validation ConditionError
Both CustomerID and attributes of customer are passed and are not void.
YPM10089An assignment cannot be both for customer and attributes of customer.
AttributeTypeRequiredTable.Column
AssignmentIDstring (maximum length: 40) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.assignment_id
Used to UNIQUELY identify a pricing rule assignment. The other columns are still included in the key for backward compatible purposes. Note that existing records, which have NULL for ASSIGNMENT_ID, will have the column populated automatically by SEQ_YPM_ASSIGNMENTID whenever managePricingRuleAssignment on the record. Once populated, it should be solely used.

CustomerIDstring (maximum length: 40) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.customer_id
The identifier for the customer. If null then this assignment is applicable irrespective of the CustomerID of a customer. If a CustomerID is passed then this assignment is based on customer identifier and hence all other customer attributes of this assignment are reset to null.

Validation ConditionError
For the passed CustomerID, customer does not exists.
YPM10028Invalid CustomerID.
The customer with passed CustomerID is not a business customer.
YPM10090Pricing rule can only be assigned to business customer.

CustomerLevelstring (maximum length: 40) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.customer_level
The level of a customer with the enterprise. If null then this assignment is applicable irrespective of the customer level of a customer.
CustomerTypestring (maximum length: 2) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.customer_type
Type of customer to which this pricing rule is assigned. If the CustomerType is null then this assignment is applicable irrespective of the type of customer. If the assignment is assigned to everyone, then all other customer attributes of this assignment are reset to null. If neither CustomerID nor attributes of customer have any specific values, then CustomerType is set to 04 i.e. assigned to everyone.

Validation ConditionError
If not null then CustomerType must be one of the allowed values.
YPM10027Invalid CustomerType.

Allowed ValueDescription
01 Indicates that this assignment is for business customer. 
02 Indicates that this assignment is for consumer customer. 
03 Indicates that this assignment is for anonymous customer. 
04 Indicates that this assignment is for everyone.
