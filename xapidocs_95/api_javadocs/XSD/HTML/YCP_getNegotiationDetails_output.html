fraction digits: 6) 		yfs_response_line_dtl.price

total number of digits: 14
fraction digits: 4) 		yfs_response_line_dtl.quantity
New quantity offered.
SequenceNononNegativeInteger (total number of digits: 5) yfs_response_line_dtl.sequence_no
One up number. Negotiation_line_key, option_no and sequence_no makes a unique key. Total quantity of the negotiation line can be split into multiple offer. For example, for total quantity of 10, oraganization's offer can be quantity=8, Price=10 and quantity=2, Price=11. In this case the two records will have sequence number 1 and 2.
