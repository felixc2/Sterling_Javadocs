Valid values - RECEIPT , VAS , PACK , OUTBOUND_INVENTORY_CONTAINER_BUILD , COUNT , SHIPPING_CONTAINER_BUILD , SHIP , INSPECTION , OUTBOUND_PICKING and REPLENISHMENT .
