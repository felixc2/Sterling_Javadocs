ElementsLoadShipmentCharge
LoadShipmentCharge Associated With Table: YFS_LOAD_SHIPMENT_CHARGE
This table stores load level and shipment level charges. Based on the existence of either load key or the shipment key on a record, a charge is associated with a load or shipment respectively. Charges can be grouped based on charge category and charge name. The invoice corresponding to the charge is posted on each record by the createShipmentInvoice API.
