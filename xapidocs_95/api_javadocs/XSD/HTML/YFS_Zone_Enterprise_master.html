StorageConstraint Associated With Table: YFS_STORAGE_CONSTRAINT
This table stores one or more records indicating what items or category of items may be stored in a zone/location. This could also be imagined to as having dedication information for a zone/location for an item, product line, velocity, catalog organization, etc. While doing put away, these constraints are used to determine the zone to stow away received inventory. Zones or locations with no storage constraints can store any inventory.

Zone Associated With Table: YFS_ZONE
This table stores the zone level setup details for a warehouse. The warehouse may be divided into multiple zones based on the product storage, packaging types, etc. Zone is a way of classifying a set of locations that share common properties. Locations belong to zones. Zone level storage constraints are auto propagated to locations through membership in a zone. The mix and do not mix constraints at zone level, apply to every single location in the zone.

MixCountryOfOriginstring (maximum length: 1) yfs_zone.mix_country_of_origin
This field indicates whether the items with different country of origin can be mixed in a location within the zone. Valid values are: Allow only if license plated: This indicates that the items with different country of origin will be mixed in this zone, only if they are license plated. Warehouses with most of their items license plated will choose this option. Always allow: Indicates that items with different country of origin, whether license plated or not, would always be mixed in this zone. Some warehouses may choose to always allow mixing for want of real estate. A physical separation may be used to differentiate between inventory with different country of origin within that zone. Never allow: Indicates that items with different country of origin will never be mixed in this zone.

TrackCaseLPNflag (maximum length: 1) yfs_zone.track_carton_lpn
This flag indicates whether the inventory is tracked in this zone. Typically, in forward pick zone, the business process may mandate that inventory should not be tracked at license plate level to avoid extra scans from the user at every pick. Otherwise, the user would need to scan the license plate for every unit picked to specify the license plate from where the stock was picked up.
TrackInventoryflag (maximum length: 1) yfs_zone.track_inventory
This flag indicates whether the inventory is tracked in this zone. In instances where a warehouse has a manufacturing plant attached to it, and it is required that there be a hand-off of inventory between these two systems, then the WMS application will simply move inventory to a location in this zone, and automatically move inventory out of the system.

ZoneEnterprise Associated With Table: YFS_ZONE_ENTERPRISE
This table defines different enterprises that store inventory in a given zone. One or more enterprise's inventory can be stored in a zone. However, whether inventory from two different enterprises can be mixed in a location depends on the mix enterprise attribute on the zone, which is propagated to every location of the zone.
