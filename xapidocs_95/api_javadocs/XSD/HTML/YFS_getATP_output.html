total number of digits: 14
fraction digits: 4) 		.
AccumulatedShortage represents the accumulated shortages within the period between the FromDate and EndDate. It is calculated as the sum of the shortage demands (with ship date earlier than or on the end date of the period).

ProjectedOnhandQtydecimal (total number of digits: 14
fraction digits: 4) 		.
ProjectedOnhandQty represents the projected on-hand quantity within the period between the FromDate and EndDate. The ProjectedOnhandQty is calculated as the sum of available quantity of all supplies (within the period). Where the available quantity of each supply is calculated as the supply quantity minus the matched demand quantity of demands earlier than or on the end of the period. Available quantity also excludes demands earlier than start date of the period.

TagControlstring (maximum length: 1) yfs_item.tag_control_flag
This field indicates whether an item is tag controlled. Inventory module uses this flag to determine if additional inventory attributes need to be stored for the item.
Allowed ValueDescription
Y Indicates that the item is always tag controlled. In this case, tag details are mandatory. 
N Indicates that the item is never tag controlled. 
S Indicates that the item is sometimes tag controlled. In this case, tag details are not mandatory.
