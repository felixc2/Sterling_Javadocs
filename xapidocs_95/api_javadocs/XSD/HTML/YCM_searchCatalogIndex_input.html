Term
AttributeTypeRequiredTable.Column
Conditionstring (maximum length: 40) .
The condition to indicate whether an item in the index is returned based on the Value being found during the search.
Valid values:
SHOULD - indicates that the items should have the specified value.
MUST - indicates that the items must have the specified value.
MUST_NOT - indicates that the items must not have the specified value. Due to limitation in Lucene, the API may not return the correct result if only MUST_NOT term(s) are passed without other terms.

SHOULD - returns documents that match the specified term. When combined with another term having a condition of SHOULD a superset is returned where documents will have one or both of the terms.
MUST - limits results only to documents that have the term specified. When combined with another MUST term, only the intersection of documents with both terms will be returned. When combined with a term with a condition of SHOULD, only items with the MUST term will be returned, with items matching the SHOULD term being prioritized in the result set.
MUST_NOT - prevents documents that match the term from being returned. For example, if Term A MUST be included in the results, and Term B MUST_NOT be included. All items with Term A will be returned with the exception of those also having Term B.

LastTermstring (maximum length: 40) .
The last term value that will be used for spelling suggestions. When the search is executed the Lucene search engine will provide suggestions for corrected spellings of the word(s) contained in the LastTerm attribute if it is found that the word(s) provided may be spelled incorrectly or there are similar words that are contained in the index. The suggestions are obtained based on the contents of item descriptions, which are stored in the Item.Description field in the index.
