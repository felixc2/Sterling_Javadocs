SelectionCriteria Associated With Table: YPM_PRICING_RULE_ASSIGNMENT
Attributes of customer used for selecting the pricing entities. If this element is not passed and CustomerID is passed then the attributes of the customer are defaulted from the customer's profile. Defaulting of PersonInfo from the customer's profile for different customer type is as below:
Type of Customer Default for PersonInfo
Business Default sold to address, if void then default bill to address.
Consumer Default bill to address.
Anonymous No defaults.
AttributeTypeRequiredTable.Column
CustomerLevelstring (maximum length: 40) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.customer_level
The level of a customer with the enterprise.
CustomerTypestring (maximum length: 2) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.customer_type
Type of customer. If neither CustomerID nor CustomerType are passed then CustomerType is defaulted to 03 .

Validation ConditionError
If not void then CustomerType must be one of the allowed values.
YPM10027Invalid CustomerType.

Allowed ValueDescription
01 Indicates that the customer is a business. 
02 Indicates that the customer is a consumer. 
03 Indicates that the customer is anonymous. 

RegionKeykey (maximum length: 24) ypm_pricing_rule_assignment.region_key
The region of the customer. If RegionKey is void then PersonInfo element is used to determine region of the customer. If both RegionKey and PersonInfo are not void then RegionKey takes precedence over PersonInfo to determine region of the customer.

Validation ConditionError
If not void then RegionKey should be a valid region within the region schema that is configured for purpose SELLING .
YCP0158Invalid region passed.

The type of the relationship that exists between an organization and an enterprise.
