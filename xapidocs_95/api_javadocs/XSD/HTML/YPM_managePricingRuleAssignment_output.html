Customer Associated With Table: YFS_CUSTOMER
This table stores customer definitions as defined by an organization for a buyer organization, a receiving node, or a consumer.
AttributeTypeRequiredTable.Column
AggregateStatusstring.
AggregateStatus is the resulting Status for the customer or contact after taking into consideration the Status of its customer hierarchy. It will only go up the hierarchy to compare statuses. The highest Status value (10,20,30) will always take priority. Examples: If the customer's Status is 10 (Active) and parent's Status is 30 (Inactive), then the current customer's AggregateStatus will be 30. If the customer's Status is 20 (On Hold), parent's Status is 10 (Active), and child's Status is 30 (Inactive), then the customer's AggregateStatus will be 20.
