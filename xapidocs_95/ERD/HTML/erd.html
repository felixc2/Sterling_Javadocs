Entity Relationship Diagram
Tables used by applications, and their relationships are available for reference. The tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications. History tables have not been shown in these relationships, as they are identical to their non-history counterparts.
Index of all Tables and Views
Business Process Platform
Barcode Translation, Productivity, Print Architecture

These tables store business process modeling data such as pipelines, transactions, service definitions and other rules. These tables also store the participant model, including organization, nodes and their related rules.
Order Management
Distributed Order Management, Supply Collaboration, Reverse Logistics

These tables store orders of all document types, such as sales order, purchase order, return order and template order.
Catalog Management
These tables store item definitions, master catalogs, categories and item classifications.
Global Inventory Visibility
These tables store supply, demand, and configuration rules that define the different supply types, demand types, etc.
Logistics Management
These tables store delivery plans, loads, shipments, containers and shipping documents such as manifests.
Warehouse Management
Warehouse Layout, Task Management, Receiving, Putaway, Inventory, Count, Move Request, Pick Planning, VAS

These tables store warehouse management data such as warehouse layout and inventory information, tasks, putaway rules, etc.
Value Added Service
These tables store service information and execution details of the service.
Pricing Service
These tables store pricing related data such as price list, pricing rules, coupons, etc.
Item Configurator Service
These tables store item's saved configuration information.
PrintExtensibility and Master Upload
These tables store information for the Print Extensibility and Master Upload Features.
IBM Sterling Applications
IBM Sterling Applications

These tables store data specific to Sterling Applications.
