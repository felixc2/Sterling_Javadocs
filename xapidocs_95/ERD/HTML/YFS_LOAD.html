Entity Definition
YFS_LOAD
( TRANSACTION Table )
Stores information about loads. Loads are made of Shipments, carry one or more shipments between two points, and carry an entire shipment. A load has one origin, one destination and can have multiple intermediate stops. A load has one carrier. Loads may or may not belong to a Delivery Plan. A load can pickup a shipment from its origin or an intermediate stop and drop off the shipment to its destination or an intermediate stop. A load is created and managed by the manageLoad API.

Records purged from this table are archived into YFS_LOAD_H

You can extend this table by adding columns and by creating hang-off table(s). Please refer to the Customization Guide for detailed procedures and restrictions.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
