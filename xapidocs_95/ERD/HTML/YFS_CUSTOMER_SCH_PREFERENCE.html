Entity Definition
YFS_CUSTOMER_SCH_PREFERENCE
( MASTER Table )
This table stores customer preferences for scheduling orders.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.

  • 01: Indicates that scheduling will first optimize on the date, then on priority, and finally on number of shipments.
  • 02: Indicates that scheduling will first optimize on priority, then on number of shipments, and finally on date.
  • 03: Indicates that scheduling will first optmize on number of shipments, then on priority, and finally on date.
