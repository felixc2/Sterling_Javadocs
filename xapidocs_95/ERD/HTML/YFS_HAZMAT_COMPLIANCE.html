Entity Definition
YFS_HAZMAT_COMPLIANCE
( CONFIGURATION Table )
This table stores hazardous material compliance data as published by the US Department of Transportation (DOT). This data is pertinent to code of federal regulations as formulated by the US Department of Transportation (DOT).



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.

Columns
Column NameData TypeDescription
HAZMAT_COMPLIANCE_KEYChar (24)The primary key for the YFS_HAZMAT_COMPLIANCE table.  
SYMBOLSChar (8)Indicates the symbol of hazardous material. 
PROPER_SHIPPING_NAMEVarchar2 (512)Indicates the proper shipping name or description of hazardous material. 
HAZARD_CLASSChar (15)Indicates the category of a hazard assigned to a hazardous material. Its value ranges from 1 to 9. 
UN_NUMBERChar (10)UN numbers are four-digit numbers that identify hazardous substances and products (such as explosives and poisonous materials) of commercial importance. This is widely used in international commerce, for instance to label the contents of shipping containers. 
PACKING_GROUPChar (5)Packing Groups (PG) indicate the degree of danger of a hazardous material within its hazard class or division and relate to the strength of packaging required for shipment. It can take values Packing Group I - high danger, Packing Group II - medium danger, Packing Group III - low danger. 
LABELChar (30)Indicates the label code. 
SPECIAL_PROVISIONSVarchar2 (100)Indicates the special provisions to be provided while shipping the hazardous material. 
EXCEPTIONVarchar2 (15)Indicates the packing exceptions while shipping the hazardous material. 
QTY_NON_BULKVarchar2 (15)Indicates the max quantity that can be shipped while shipping non bulk. 
QTY_BULKVarchar2 (15)Indicates the max quantity that can be shipped while shipping bulk. 
PASSENGER_AIRVarchar2 (15)Indicates the max quantity that can be shipped while shipping via passenger aircraft or rail. 
CARGO_AIRVarchar2 (15)Indicates the max quantity that can be shipped while shipping via cargo aircraft. 
VESSELChar (5)Specifies where the cargo is stowed on a vessel. 
VESSEL_SPChar (35)Specifies other stowage plans. 
SORT_ORDERNumber (6,0)Unique number to identify a hazardous substance. The difference between the preceding and the succeeding sort order value equals to five. This is to accommodate new hazardous materials. 
LOCKIDNumber (5,0)An integer used to track and prevent concurrent modifications.
