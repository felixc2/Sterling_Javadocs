Entity Definition
YCS_INTERNATIONAL_RECORD
( TRANSACTION Table )
This table stores UPS international shipper information.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.

Columns
Column NameData TypeDescription
INTERNATIONAL_RECORD_KEYChar (24)The primary key for the YCS_INTERNATIONAL_RECORD table.  
MANIFEST_NUMBERChar (30)This will have the MANIFEST_UPS_DTL_KEY of the shipper's carton record in YCS_MANIFEST_UPS_DTL table. 
INVOICE_DATEDateThe ship time stamp. 
WAYBILL_PRINT_INDChar (1)Indicates if Waybill was produced for the shipment. As per UPS specification, valid values are 1 (Waybill was produced for shipment), default value is 0(No waybill used for shipment). 
INVOICE_LINE_TOTALSNumber (22,4)The value of the goods determined by shipper, as indicated on the waybill or commercial invoice.This is the sum of all LINE_EXTENDED_AMT in YCS_COMMODITY_RECORD table for the shipment. 
INVOICE_CURRENCY_CODEChar (3)The currency code used for INVOICE_LINE_TOTALS. This must conform to IATA/ISO country codes. 
SHIPMENTINSURANCEDECLAREDVALUENumber (22,4)The value for goods that is determined by shipper for insurance purpose. 
CONSOLIDATED_CLEAR_QTYNumber (22,4)The number of consignees in the consolidated clearance. 
ULTIMATE_DEST_COUNTRYChar (2)The country of ultimate destination, as indicated on the waybill or SED. As per UPS specification, this is required when an Ultimate Consignee address is completed on the S.E.D. 
SED_CODEChar (1)This indicates if the Shipper's Export Declaration has been completed or not. As per UPS specification, this field is currently used for US and PR origins only; valid values are D(S.E.D. included with export documents), E(Electronically filed by the shipper pre-departure with an AES transaction number), P(Electronically filed by the shipper post-departure), U(UPS-prepared on the shipper's behalf), Y(S.E.D. exists but type unknown), space (Not applicable). 
SHIPMENT_SED_CAS_NUMVarchar2 (10)As per UPS specification this field should be set to spaces. 
INVOICE_NUMBERChar (35)The invoice number as indicated on the invoice/waybill. 
PO_NUMBERChar (35)The Purchase Order Number as indicated on invoice/waybill. 
DESCRIPTION_OF_GOODSVarchar2 (50)A general description of the goods contained in the shipment. 
SPECIAL_INSTRUCTIONSVarchar2 (69)Shipper's comments or special instructions. 
PARTIES_TO_TRANSChar (1)If SED is applicable, it indicates whether shipper and consignee/importer are related or non-related.If not applicable, this is set to space. As per UPS specification, this is currently used for US and PR origins only; valid values are R(related), N(not related), space(not applicable). 
TERMS_OF_SHIPMENTChar (3)Terms of delivery as indicated on the commercial invoice. As per UPS specification, valid values are CFR(Cost and Freight), CIF(Cost Insurance and Freight), CIP(Carriage and Insurance Paid), CPT(Carriage Paid To), DAF(Delivered At Frontier), DDP(Delivered Duty Paid), DDU(Delivered Duty Unpaid), DEQ(Delivered Ex Quay), DES(Delivered Ex Ship), EXW(Ex Works), FAS(Free Along Side), FCA(Free Carrier), FOB(Free on Board), Space(Not applicable). 
PAYMENT_TERMSChar (35)Description of the payment terms. 
FREIGHT_CHARGESNumber (22,4)The freight amount as indicated on the commercial invoice. 
INSURANCE_CHARGESNumber (22,4)The Insurance Amount as indicated on the commercial invoice. 
DISCOUNT_REBATENumber (22,4)Discount or Rebate amount as indicated on the commercial invoice. 
OTHER_CHARGESNumber (22,4)Sum of the other amounts being charged as indicated on the commercial invoice. 
WAYBILLNUMBER_BROKERAGEIDVarchar2 (11)The waybill/brokerage Identification Number. 
COCODEChar (1)This field indicates if Certificate of Origin was completed or not, and the form it was on. As per UPS specification, this field is currently used for US and PR origins only; valid values are Space(No Certificate of Origin created), D(US Certificate of Origin created through shipping system and included with export documents), N(NAFTA Certificate of Origin created through shipping system and included with export documents, U(US Certificate of Origin prepared by UPS on shippers behalf). 
OTHER_DOC_CODEChar (1)This indicates if any additional document is attached to the shipper. As per UPS specification, valid values are 0(no documents attached), 1(documents are attached), space(not applicable). 
REASON_FOR_EXPORTChar (35)The reason the shipment is to be exported as indicated on the commercial invoice. 
INVOICE_SUB_TOTALNumber (22,4)This is the result of subtracting DISCOUNT_REBATE from INVOICE_LINE_TOTALS. 
TOTAL_INVOICE_AMOUNTNumber (22,4)The sum of INVOICE_LINE_TOTALS, DISCOUNT_REBATE, FREIGHT_CHARGES, INSURANCE_CHARGES and OTHER_CHARGES. 
BROKER_CODEChar (3)Identifies the broker for the shipment. 
DESTINATION_CONTROLVarchar2 (200)The destination control statement,as per UPS specification. 
SHIPMENT_COMMODITY_ORIGINChar (2)The country code that identifies the country of origin for the commodities. 
DECLARATION_STATEMENTVarchar2 (256)A statement or statements from the shipper declaring certain facts or criteria to be true. 
ADDITIONAL_COMMENTSVarchar2 (256)Comments or remarks directed from the shipper to the consignee. 
LOCKIDNumber (5,0)An integer used to track and prevent concurrent modifications. 
CREATETSDateTimeTimestamp when the record was created. 
MODIFYTSDateTimeTimestamp when the record was last modified. 
CREATEUSERIDVarchar2 (40)User who created the record. 
MODIFYUSERIDVarchar2 (40)User who modified the record. 
CREATEPROGIDVarchar2 (40)Program that created the record. 
MODIFYPROGIDVarchar2 (40)Program that modified the record.
